How to jailbreak Apple devices

If you are on iOS 4:
Use Greenpois0n to jailbreak.
How to use greenpois0n:
Connect your device in DFU Mode and then click "Jailbreak".
If you are on iOS 5:
Then use redsn0w to jailbreak.
How to use redsn0w to jailbreak:
In the main page of redsn0w click "Jailbreak"
to Jailbreak.
Then select only the option called Cydia and then click "Next".
Sometimes it will do the Rocky Racoon Jailbreak and when it does
that it doesn't ask you to enter the option called Cydia.
If you are on iOS 6.1.3 or higher:
Then use p0sixpwn to jailbreak.
How to use p0sixpwn:
Connect your device in normal mode and then click "Jailbreak".
If you are on iOS 7 or higher:
Then use 3uTools to Jailbreak.
How to use 3uTools:
Click "Toolbox" then click "Jailbreak" then Select the jailbreak and then click "Start Jailbreak".
Congrats, you have jailbreaked your device!
